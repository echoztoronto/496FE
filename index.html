A monitoring and forecast system for distributed energy resources using deep learning


The goal of this project is to establish a system for energy demand monitoring and forecasting using deep learning. A front-end user interface will be implemented, in order to provide potential users with anomaly identifications based on the forecast predictions, where areas with an abnormally high or low level of energy are being consumed are flagged based on forecast predictions. If time permits, we will conduct experiments on the climatic effects on a grid, with synthetic data based on various on predicted demand fluctuations due to climate change.
